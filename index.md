# Privacy Policy for Provident Prep

**Last updated: March 2026**

---

## Overview

Provident Prep ("the App") is a free app built to help individuals and families manage food storage and emergency preparedness. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

---

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
Provident Prep stores data you enter directly, including:
- Food storage items (names, quantities, categories, expiration dates)
- 72-hour kit checklist progress
- Goals and preferences you set within the App

This data is stored **locally on your device** and, if you choose to enable iCloud Sync, in your personal iCloud account.

### Camera Access
The App requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning barcodes to identify and add food items. Photos are not taken, stored, or transmitted. Camera access is only used during active barcode scanning sessions.

---

## How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide the features of Provident Prep:
- Displaying and organizing your food storage inventory
- Tracking 72-hour kit checklist progress
- Generating rotation reminders based on expiration dates you enter

We do **not** use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the core functionality of the App.

---

## Data Storage & iCloud Sync

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you enable iCloud Sync in your device's iCloud settings, your data will sync across your Apple devices through your personal iCloud account. This sync is managed entirely by Apple and is subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third parties.

The App uses the [Open Food Facts API](https://world.openfoodfacts.org) to look up product information when you scan a barcode. Only the barcode number is sent to this service during a lookup — no personal information is included. Open Food Facts is a free, open-source food database. Their privacy policy is available at [https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy).

---

## Notifications

The App may send local notifications to remind you about upcoming food rotation dates. These notifications are generated on-device and do not involve any external servers or data transmission.

---

## Children's Privacy

Provident Prep does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App contains no user accounts, registration, or data collection mechanisms.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on this page with an updated date.
